Pi Network is a mobile-based blockchain project aiming to make cryptocurrency accessible to everyone. It utilizes a unique "mining" process that can be done on your smartphone, supposedly without significant battery drain.

What is Pi?

  • A new digital currency (Pi) designed for everyday people.
  • Built on a decentralized blockchain network, aiming to be secure and transparent.

How does it work?

  • Users ("Pioneers") "mine" Pi through the Pi app on their phones.
  • Mining contributes to the network's security by verifying transactions and growing a trusted network.
  • Increase mining rate by inviting friends ("Ambassadors") and running personal nodes.

Key Features and Benefits

  • Mobile Mining: Innovative technology that doesn't drain your battery.
  • Decentralized and Secure: Immutable transactions and a meritocratic system.
  • Environmental Consciousness: Eco-friendly mining.
